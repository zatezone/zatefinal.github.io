STRANGER THINGS (2016 - 2024)




Set in the 1980s, the series centers around the residents of the fictional small town of Hawkins, Indiana, as they are plagued by a hostile alternate dimension known as the "Upside Down", after a nearby human experimentation facility opens a gateway between the Upside Down and the normal world.

GENRE: Horror, Horror fiction, Thriller, Drama, Supernatural, Mystery, Costume drama
DIRECTOR: Matt Duffer & Ross Duffer

WEDNESDAY (2022)




Follows Wednesday Addams' years as a student, when she attempts to master her emerging psychic ability, thwart a killing spree, and solve the mystery that embroiled her parents. A sleuthing, supernaturally infused mystery charting Wednesday Addams' years as a student at Nevermore Academy.

GENRE: Supernatural fiction, Fantasy, Comedy horror, Supernatural, Coming-of-age story
DIRECTOR: Tim Burton
